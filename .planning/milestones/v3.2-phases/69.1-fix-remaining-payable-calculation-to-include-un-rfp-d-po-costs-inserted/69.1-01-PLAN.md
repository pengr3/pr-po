<objective>
Fix three bugs in the Remaining Payable scoreboard card in app/expense-modal.js:

1. Wrong formula: remainingPayable = totalRequested - totalPaid only covers RFPd amounts. Fix to totalCost - totalPaid so un-RFPd PO costs are included.
2. Voided payments inflating totalPaid: payment_records are summed without filtering out status voided records. Fix to match finance.js pattern.
3. Render gate hides card when no RFPs exist: totalRequested > 0 condition prevents the card from showing for projects with POs but no RFPs. Fix to totalCost > 0.

Purpose: Users see the true remaining unpaid cost for a project/service, not just the RFP-covered portion.
Output: Corrected app/expense-modal.js with all three fixes applied.
</objective>
