## One-Liner

Fixed Remaining Payable formula to use totalCost minus non-voided totalPaid, ensuring un-RFP'd PO costs are included and the card renders for projects with POs but zero RFPs.

## What Was Changed

### Fix 1 — Remaining Payable formula (app/expense-modal.js)
- Changed `remainingPayable = totalRequested - totalPaid` → `remainingPayable = totalCost - totalPaid`
- This ensures un-RFP'd PO costs are included in what's owed, not just what was requested

### Fix 2 — Exclude voided payments from totalPaid (app/expense-modal.js)
- Added filter to exclude `payment_records` with voided status before summing totalPaid
- Matches the existing pattern in finance.js

### Fix 3 — Render gate change (app/expense-modal.js)
- Changed render condition from `totalRequested > 0` to `totalCost > 0`
- Projects with POs but zero RFPs filed now correctly show the Remaining Payable card
