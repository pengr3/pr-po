## Decisions Made

- Em dash (`\u2014`) used as visual separator between tranche label and paid percentage for clean readability
- Integer rounding (`Math.round`) chosen over `toFixed(1)` — whole numbers are sufficient for this display
- Guard condition `totalPaid > 0 && totalAmount > 0` ensures pending POs are not affected and division-by-zero is prevented
