<objective>
Tighten the Projects and Services chart cards on the home page so the horizontal bar chart visually dominates the card. Currently the `.hs-chart-canvas.hs-chart-status` wrapper is 320px tall (360px on mobile) while the bar itself is only 22px — the bar looks tiny and lost in empty space. Reduce wrapper height, bump bar thickness, and enlarge the legend text + color squares by 2px so the chart fills its container proportionally.

Purpose: Visual polish — make the home dashboard feel proportional and intentional rather than half-empty.

Output:
- `styles/views.css` — reduced `.hs-chart-canvas.hs-chart-status` height (320px → 180px desktop, 360px → 220px mobile)
- `app/views/home.js` — Chart.js legend `boxWidth`/`boxHeight`/`font.size` raised from 10 → 12, `barThickness` raised from 22 → 28
</objective>
