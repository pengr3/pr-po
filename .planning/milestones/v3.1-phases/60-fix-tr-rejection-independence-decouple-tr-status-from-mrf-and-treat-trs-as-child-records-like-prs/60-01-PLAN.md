<objective>
Stop TR rejection and TR approval from cascading status changes onto the parent MRF document. A TR is a child record — its lifecycle must be fully independent. Also surface prior rejection context in the Finance TR review modal when a resubmitted TR is opened.

Purpose: MRFs that have approved PRs must never become re-editable due to a TR rejection. The MRF approval is a one-way door — TR outcomes do not affect MRF status.
Output: finance.js with two surgical removals (MRF update blocks) and one addition (prior rejection notice in TR modal).
</objective>
