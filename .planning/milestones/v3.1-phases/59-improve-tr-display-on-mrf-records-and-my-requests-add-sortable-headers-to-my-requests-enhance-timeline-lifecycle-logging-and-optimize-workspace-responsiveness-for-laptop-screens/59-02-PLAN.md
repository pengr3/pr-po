<objective>
Enhance the Procurement Timeline modal in both procurement.js and mrf-records.js to display the full rejection and resubmission lifecycle for PRs and TRs, with color-coded event types showing reason, actor, and timestamp.

Purpose: Currently the timeline shows PR/TR items as a single entry regardless of rejection history. When Finance rejects a PR or TR and Procurement resubmits, this lifecycle is invisible. Users cannot tell why something was rejected or that it was resubmitted.
Output: Timeline modal shows color-coded events: created (blue-grey), submitted (blue-grey), rejected (red, with reason + actor + timestamp), resubmitted (orange/yellow), approved (green), with PO children nested under approved PRs.
</objective>

1. "Transport Request Submitted" event (always) — class based on current finance_status
