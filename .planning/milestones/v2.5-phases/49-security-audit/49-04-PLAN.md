## Goal
All `console.log` and `console.info` statements are removed from the codebase to prevent leaking internal state, PII, or debug data in production. Auth module gets the strictest treatment (console.error only).

## Context
- 206 console.log/info statements across 23 files to remove
- ~200 console.warn/error statements to KEEP (these are actionable)
- auth.js gets strictest treatment: remove ALL console statements except console.error (29 total, 13 are warn/error)

## Decisions (from CONTEXT.md — non-negotiable)
- Remove all `console.log` and `console.info` across the entire app
- Keep `console.warn` and `console.error` for actionable signals
- Auth module: remove ALL console statements except `console.error`
