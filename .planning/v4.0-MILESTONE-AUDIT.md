# v4.0 Milestone Audit — Procurement → Full Management Portal

**Audited:** 2026-06-16 · **Branch:** `v3.3` (milestone internally numbered **v4.0**; ships as v3.3 → main) · **Status:** `gaps_found`

This milestone transforms CLMC from a procurement tool into a full management portal across five capability areas: Project Management (Gantt/Tasks), In-App Notifications, Collectibles Tracking, Proposal Lifecycle, and a Management hub. Scope: all phases ≥ 83 (v3.2 ended at 82) — **53 phase directories**, 51 active requirements.

> **Headline:** Every one of the 51 requirements is *delivered in code* and SUMMARY-confirmed — **0 orphaned, 0 never-built**. The milestone is held back from a clean pass by **2 confirmed integration blockers** in the notification↔proposal wiring (B1, B2) and a systemic **verification-rigor gap** (15 phases, incl. the 3 foundational ones, never produced a passing VERIFICATION.md; live UAT was deferred on ~26 phases). These are closeable with a small, well-scoped gap phase — not a rebuild.
