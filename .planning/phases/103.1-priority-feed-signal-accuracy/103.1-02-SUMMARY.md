## Exact placement of each bump
- `postActivityEntry` (~3033): `const ok = await _addActivityEntry(...)` → `if (ok) { updateDoc(projectRef,{last_activity_at:serverTimestamp()}).catch(...) }`
- `submitProgressUpdate` (~3220): bump immediately after the `await addDoc(progress_updates)` resolves, before `journalProgressFormOpen = false`
- `submitNewIssue` (~3417): bump immediately after the `await addDoc(issues)` resolves, before `journalIssueFormOpen = false`
- `_addActivityEntry` (~3045): `return true;` after addDoc resolves, `return false;` in catch (non-rethrow preserved)

All three bumps: NOT awaited, NOT batched with the addDoc, `.catch`-swallowed. The D-03 landmine holds — a non-team active user's denied parent write cannot roll back the journal entry.
