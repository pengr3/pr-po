After the line `let displayed = rfpsData;` and BEFORE the existing rfpStatusFilter/rfpDeptFilter blocks, add a default exclusion that filters out Fully Paid RFPs UNLESS the user has explicitly selected "Fully Paid" in the status dropdown:

// Default: hide Fully Paid RFPs unless explicitly filtered to show them
