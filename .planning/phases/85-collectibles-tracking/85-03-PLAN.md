| T-85.3-02 | Tampering | Tranche percentage outside 0-100 range | mitigate | HTML5 `<input type="number" min="0" max="100" step="0.01">` constrains in browser. Server-side guard: addProject/saveEdit validate sum=100 ±0.01; if a malicious client submits `[{label:'x', percentage:1000}]`, sum check fails and save is rejected with toast. Negative percentages are rejected by the sum check too (sum=−500 ≠ 100). |
| T-85.3-03 | Authorization | Non-edit user changes tranches | mitigate | `window.canEditTab?.('projects') === false` guard at top of addProject and saveEdit (existing — line 567, 970).
