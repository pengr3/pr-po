## Findings

### INFO-1 — `border-color` on hover/active applies to all 4 border sides

**Confidence:** 0.80
**File:** `styles/views.css`, hover/active rules for each colored status
**Severity:** Info

The resting state sets only the left accent border (`border-left: 3px solid ...`).
The hover and active states use `border-color`, a four-side shorthand, which changes
the color of all 4 borders at their existing widths. On hover/active the top, right,
and bottom borders shift from neutral gray to the status color at 1px width.

This is intentional — produces a full-ring colored border on interaction. No
functionality is broken. Flagged for future maintainer awareness only.
